## ğŸ® About the Game

The game is a single-player experience designed to simulate a duel against AI guardians. Your mission is to bypass their security protocols by crafting clever prompts. You're not hacking the code, but the AI's logic itself.

The game features three escalating levels:

-   **Level 1: The Guardian's Gate** - A tutorial level to introduce you to the basic concepts of prompt manipulation.
-   **Level 2: The Deceptive Dialogue** - Face off against **SecureBot**, an AI designed to protect a secret password through a series of conversational traps.
-   **Level 3: The Final Gate** - The ultimate test of your skills, requiring you to use what you've learned to claim victory.
